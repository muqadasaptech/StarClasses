AMAZON VIRTUAL ASSISTANT

Amazon's virtual assistant, known as Alexa, is an advanced voice-activated AI system designed to facilitate a wide range of tasks through natural language interaction. Launched in 2014, Alexa is embedded in various devices, including the Amazon Echo series, smart speakers, and even some third-party gadgets. Users can engage with Alexa to play music, control smart home devices, check the weather, set reminders, and answer queries, among many other functionalities. Its capabilities continue to expand through regular updates and the addition of new "skills," which are essentially voice-driven apps that enable Alexa to perform specialized tasks or provide specific information.
The integration of Alexa into the Internet of Things (IoT) ecosystem further enhances its utility, allowing users to manage various smart home devices seamlessly. This interoperability makes it possible to control lights, thermostats, and security systems through simple voice commands, thereby increasing convenience and energy efficiency.
